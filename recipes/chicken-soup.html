Chicken Soup

Description:

Delve into the heartwarming embrace of a classic chicken noodle soup, where fragrant herbs, tender chicken, and wholesome vegetables create a symphony of satisfaction in every mouthful. The golden elixir of the broth tantalizes the senses, while succulent pieces of chicken and perfectly cooked noodles add a wholesome base to the medley of flavors. Fresh vegetables infuse the broth with sweetness and vibrancy, and expertly blended herbs and spices create a memorable culinary experience. This delightful chicken noodle soup is more than just a dish; it's a comforting embrace, a nostalgic memory, and a soul-nourishing treasure that warms the heart and delights the taste buds.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken Broth
  • Garlic bouillon
  • Chicken bouillon
  • Garlic
  • Peas
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Onion
  • Lemon
  • Ginger
  • Umami seasoning
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive oil
  • Egg noodles
  • Rotisserie chicken

Steps:

  1. Heat 2 boxes of bone broth or chicken broth on stove top in large pot on medium heat.
  2. Add 1-2 tsps of garlic bouillon and chicken bouillon.
  3. Add approx. 20 cloves of garlic, 1.5 cups of peas, carrots, celery, onions (all chopped), 1-2 tsps ginger.
  4. Season with 1-2 tsps of umami seasoning, dash of salt, pepper, and squeeze half a lemon.
  5. Stir and let reach a rolling boil, then simmer on low heat.
  6. In separate pot, boil egg noddles with a dash of salt and olive oil.
  7. Combine noodles and soup and simmer together for 10 mins.
  8. Add rotisserie chicken to soup, then pour yourself a nice bowl to eat.
